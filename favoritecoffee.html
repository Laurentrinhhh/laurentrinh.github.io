My Favorite Coffee Drinks

Latte

A smooth espresso drink with steamed milk and a light layer of foam on top. Perfect for a cozy afternoon.

Espresso

A strong, bold shot of pure coffee. The heart of all coffee drinks — simple and intense.

Cappuccino

A balanced mix of espresso, steamed milk, and foam — often dusted with cocoa or cinnamon.

Iced Coffee

Refreshing and cool. Brewed coffee poured over ice — enjoy it black or with milk and syrup.
